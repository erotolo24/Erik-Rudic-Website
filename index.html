A LITTLE BIT ABOUT ME:

Hi, I’m Erik Rudic and I’ve been playing guitar professionally for 15 years. I teach guitar, bass, piano, recording, and music production. In addition to teaching I’ve toured the world with notable artists such as Peter Criss (Kiss), Nena, The Parlor Mob, Nicole Atkins, and Gedeon Luke. In 2015 I appeared with Gedeon Luke on the popular UK television show Later…With Jools Holland performing alongside Robert Plant, Counting Crows, and Stormzy. Years of touring have allowed me to act as support for bands such as Guns N' Roses, Tool, Rob Zombie, and Blink-182.
